The Japanese Buddhist World Map: Religious Vision and the Cartographic Imagination

著者名: D. Max Moerman 出版者: University of Hawai"i Press 出版年: 31 Dec 2021 版表示: シリーズ: HardbackFrom the fourteenth through the nineteenth centuries Japanese monks created hundreds of maps to construct and locate their place in a Buddhist world. This expansively illustrated volume is the first to explore the largely unknown archive of Japanese Buddhist world maps and analyze their production, reproduction, and reception.
